The provided script, shasum, does not have a manpage - Debian requires a manpage for all the files installed in the /usr/bin directory. I did a small patch to your file, basically copying the 'usage' information to POD. I also escaped some backticks/apostrophes in the usage information that made it awkward to be edited in Emacs, as they broke syntax-highlighting. Thanks a lot!
