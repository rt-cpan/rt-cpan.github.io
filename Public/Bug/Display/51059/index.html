Subject: DBD::mysql incorrectly handles Unicode chars with a codepoint > ffff
DBD::mysql's 'mysql_enable_utf8' flag works fine for characters with a codepoint of 'ffff' or below - above that, it's failing to either encode or decode them properly. Attached script demonstrates.
