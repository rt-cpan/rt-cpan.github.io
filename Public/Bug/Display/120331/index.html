Hi Ian, The tests are failing on using a hashref for content on the PUT method. As it turns out, HTTP::Message (which contains HTTP::Request) prior to version 6.07 didn't allow PUT requests to use hashrefs for content. https://metacpan.org/changes/release/ETHER/HTTP-Message-6.07#L13 So, to fix, please update your HTTP::Message and re-try your LWP install. We will discuss the best way to fix this in the next release of LWP. Thank you for pointing this out to us! Thanks, Chase
