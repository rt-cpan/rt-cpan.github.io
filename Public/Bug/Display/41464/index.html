Fundamentals of Power Trading
January 21-22, 2009 - Philadelphia
Presented by the Energy Management Institute

Understand the key factors that drive success in the power markets


Get course details       Register

Are you a beginner in power trading looking the learn the essentials?

Or maybe you're more advanced and need to learn the finer points of power trading?

Then Fundamentals of Power Trading is for you.

Fundamentals of Power Trading provides basic to mid-level participants with comprehensive training on power trading. Our leading industry experts will provide comprehensive hands-on instruction, lecture and real-life examples that bring to life the entire power market complex.

Topics covered include:

  • Why are the power markets the most volatile of all markets?
  • What fundamental factors drive the power markets?
  • What role does weather play in determining prices?
  • What role do fuel prices play in determining prices?
  • What are Locational Marginal Prices and why should I care?
  • What is the difference between real time, day ahead and forward power prices?
  • What trading tools are used in the power markets, and where can participants find liquidity?
  • How are power trading books analyzed, and how do risk management best practices apply?
  • What is the status of power market de-regulation and where is it headed?
  • How do ancillary services, emissions and renewable mandates affect the power markets?
