Dear Christopher, I am aware of the problem, which has occurred not because of the change in maintainer, but of the change in version: 2.9 was actually meant to represent 2.09; when transitioning to 2.10, CPAN has considered 2.10 < 2.9 and refused to index the new version of the package. The problem should spontaneously resolve when the upcoming 2.11 is out, which is due relatively soon. I hope it's not too much a problem to wait until then. Let me know, and thanks for reporting this issue. cheers, Alessandro
