  Mon Jan 20 10:18:18 2020 SWALTERS [...] cpan.org - Correspondence added
Thank you for your report. SOAP has a governing body and working group staffed almost entirely by Microsoft and Oracle people that churns out volumes and volumes of specifications in a CORBA-esque nightmare of complexity. When you opened this ticket, SOAP::WSDL was hopelessly behind and since then has been moved to life support. Reasonable looking patches will be applied and critical toolchain problems resolved (optimistically speaking). Documentation has been updated to note that this module is hopelessly out of date. For actual SOAP work, I recommend using an module such as Inline::Java or Inline::CSharp (if such a thing exists), or just writing Java.
