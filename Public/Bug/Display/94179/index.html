Sorry for replying so late but we haven't updated CPAN yet to point to our bugtracker at https://issues.apache.org/jira/browse/LUCY The RT tickets are rarely reviewed. The behavior of RangeQuery is to sort undefined or missing fields last. This is not user-configurable at the moment. As a work-around, you could provide an upper_term limit that is larger than any field in your index. If this issue is important to you, please post a bug report on the ASF JIRA bugtracker mentioned above.
