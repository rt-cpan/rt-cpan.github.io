Beacon of hope charity organization is a registered nonprofit organization in Pretoria, Gauteng, South Africa providing support, counseling and needs to raise substantial funds, food, product and clothing to maintain the service that people who are less privileged to overcome the barriers that impede their independence and participation in the society. Beacon of Hope's two fish and five loaves, feeding the multitudes project focuses on providing healthy, nutritional meals to those individuals in our target communities, who cannot afford a proper meal on a regular basis. Our target communities include informal settlements, foster care house, safe houses and facilities for aged and impoverished communities in South Africa The project is entirely reliant on food, clothes and monetary donations by the public and corporate donors. The number of people that can be fed or clothed is totally dependent on the size of the contributions. Beacon of Hope staff is constantly seeking out new avenues for funding this project. The aim of this project is to feed as many people possible, as many times as to make a real impact on alleviating hunger and malnutrition in our target communities. Beacon of Hope's vision is to also provide quarterly food hamper to about 100 families in selected poor communities.
