I added the following to nouns.lei to get rid of "expenses" (pretenses, offenses, etc) being singularized the wrong way: -nse => | -nses -[^ns]sis => | -[^ns]ses I didn't bother with a diff, as it's literally a line. before: [dorian$pants:~] perl -MLingua::EN::Inflexion=noun -le 'print noun(shift)->singular' pretenses pretensis after: [dorian$deuce:~] perl -MLingua::EN::Inflexion=noun -le 'print noun(shift)->singular' pretenses pretense Cheers
