Subject: body is not decoded according to Content-Type= ... ; charset=...
Download (untitled) / with headers
text/plain 470b
When the body method is called, it properly decodes the content according to the Content-Transfert-Encoding , which gives a binary octet string. In case of Content-Type: text/plain; charset=UTF-8 , this method forgets to decode the binary content into a proper perl string, by doing a decode($charset , $bincontent) . I provide a little script to illustrate this an an attachement. You'll need to have your terminal set to 'UTF-8' to see the effect. Regards. Jerome.
