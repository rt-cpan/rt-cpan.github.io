The next time that pure mail is read, the very last header will be missing in E::Simple object.

The problem with -only- looking for /^From / is that traditionally Email::Folder::Mbox has tried to handle all the various types of mbox encoding, which means dealing with mboxcl, which allows the Content-Length header to skip you past unencoded body lines beginning with "From ". This is stupid, but it's how it has worked in the past. I am pretty tempted to abandon it, however, as it is needlessly complex, and not very reliable. -- rjbs
