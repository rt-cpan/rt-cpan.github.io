Subject: Broken quoted multiline handling
Text::CSV (and CSV_XS) already provide you with a proper getline method which actually reads a CSV line, which may very well pan *multiple* file-lines. As it stands this module is useless for parsing: "stuff like", "this" due to you doing the reading yourself: https://metacpan.org/source/ADAMK/Parse-CSV-2.00/lib/Parse/CSV.pm#L354
