Emmanuel, [ Just so you're aware of it ] RT 4.x allows for reporting on the selected fields (not just all fields). RT 3.8.x does not allow for this, as you have indicated above. However, there is a solution for 3.8.x TSV output at the following URL which could be integrated into your extension, since you package your own "Results.xxx" file. It's just a matter of whether or not you want to bother. See "3.8" here: http://requesttracker.wikia.com/wiki/SpreadsheetDisplayedFields
