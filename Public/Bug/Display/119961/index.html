Subject: configure(proxy_host, proxy_port), SSL and fail_on_error broken
There are patches for SSL and the proxy_host and proxy_port problem. I've rolled them up into one and included them here. The other problem is either with on_error, or something else! ;) The module will fail and quit completely after the first encounter of a problem with a URL when using concurrent or asynchronous URLs. So, if you connected fine to google.com, and then went to blah49.com and it failed, the module won't go to the next URL in queue, it just quits. I don't know if its on_error, or something else. I also tinkered with fail_on_error, but got no results with 0 or 1.
