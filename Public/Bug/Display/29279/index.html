Hello Marek, thanks for reporting this. This was due to a bug in open_proxy_tcp_connection was stopping after the first \n from teh proxy, but instead should have looked for $CRLF . $CRLF to find the beginning of the SSL content Your log shows that none of the handshakes worked. I dont understand how this could have ever worked properly in the past, since all proxy servers should return CRLF CRLF after its headers. Anyway, I have uploadd a fix to SVN. Cheers.
