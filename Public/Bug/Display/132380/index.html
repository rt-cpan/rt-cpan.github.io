I am having answers like these: 20 APPEND Folders/Test (\Seen) "09-Apr-2020 12:21:44 +0000" {3032}+ send literal * 1 EXISTS * OK [UNSEEN 0] * 1 FETCH (FLAGS (\Seen nonjunk) UID 7) 20 OK [APPENDUID 5 7] APPEND successful This coauses this line: my $ret = $data =~ m#\s+(\d+)\]# ? $1 : $self; To produce 0 from [UNSEEN 0]. Needs to be replace with a least something like: my $ret = $data =~ m#\s+(\d+)\]\s+APPEND# ? $1 : $self; But I can image more complex answers, for example spaces after id: 20 OK [APPENDUID 5 7 ] APPEND successful So maybe even: my $ret = $data =~ m#\s+(\d+)\s+\]\s+APPEND# ? $1 : $self; The current regular expression is extremely fragile.
