The Perl module files like lib/Tree.pm state: This library is free software; you can redistribute it and/or modify it under the same terms as Perl itself. <http://dev.perl.org/licenses/> defines Perl license as GPL 1 or newer or Artistic 1. But the LICENSE file quotes Artistic 2.0 license. May I ask which of the two statements follow your intention? Could you please provide a clarification?
