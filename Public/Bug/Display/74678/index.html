In January 2012 VWD aka finanztreff.de changed the format of their funds detail page a little bit. Also they introduced a throttle to limit frequent search queries. I fixed both problems in the attached VWD.pm module. Please note that due to the throttle mechanism you should avoid to query more than five items at once. Otherwise it may take a long time to get the results.
