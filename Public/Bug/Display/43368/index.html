Subject: Change explain() and show() to use note() instead of TEST_VERBOSE.
This patch removes the assumption that Test::Most is exclusively going to be used by prove and makes it a general purpose TAP writer. It means explain() diagnostics will show up in TAP archives and when you run the test by hand. Also removes the explain() dependency on a very recent Test::Harness. Also documented the difference between Test::More's explain and Test::Most.
