This probably has something to do with AWS::SQS as well, since code and keys that worked fine for a year stopped working yesterday (8/120/2015). After digging around in the Perl debugger, it looks like the 'params' and the signature calculation is failing when the secretKey has a '+' sign in it, which is probably getting interpreted as space. Fix might be urlencoding this. I generated a new keyId and secretKey that has no plus sign in it, and things work.
