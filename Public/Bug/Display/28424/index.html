It looks like a Module::Build problem, not related at all to my module. Note that ExtUtil::MakeMaker uses PREFIX=DIR variable for installing stuff locally, but Module::Build uses something like --prefix=DIR or something like that. Could it be that you need to a) update your CPAN, and b) configure the M::B setting for this? Win32::Word::Writer also needs MS Word installed, and Win32::OLE (wich may or may not work under Cygwin, but I think it may). /J
