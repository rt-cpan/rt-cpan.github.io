Subject: [PATCH] Fix building with DBI installed in local::lib
DBI migth be installed anywhere in @INC, not just the compiled-in site/vendor/core directories. So instead of trying to guess the header files are based on various %Config values, just ask DBI::DBD. See attached patch.
