Hi Marc, I'm happy for you to take on any level of involvement in the maintenance of the module; whatever you feel comfortable with. The problem is that I have very little time to devote to the maintenance of this module (or of any of my many modules). And I have even less time for extended technical collaborations. Moreover, I am just about to start an extended sabbatical, during which I will have no time at all. So if you would like to send me patches against the current CPAN distribution, that would be great (though I may be slow to review and apply them in the coming months). Or if you would like PAUSE access to the module, so you can update status on bug reports, that would be great too (and I can set that up for you immediately). Or if you just want to send me requests-for-fixes or request-for-features, that's okay too (except that I will be extremely slow to act on such requests in the near future). However, to address the original point of this thread: I don't propose to change the way I currently maintain modules or to set up public repositories for any of them. If anyone prefers that approach, I am happy for them to fork the particular module (or to take over its entire maintenance) and then to set up such things themselves. All the best, Damian
