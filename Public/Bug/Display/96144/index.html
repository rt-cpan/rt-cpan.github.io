"LEADERSHIP IN ACTION" with Dr. Marshall Goldsmith, very intensive course is based on his book "What Got You Here Won't Get You There". A one-day transformational experience for leaders looking to achieve results. Discover the ideas and trends that are shaping the future of business. The program has been been designed for a select  gathering of high-level leaders. It will provide a unique environment in which to tackle the most critical challenges facing senior business leaders today. 

Learning objectives:

  • Develop the ability and willingness of internal organizational resources to effectively coach others
  • Understand and apply a proven process to achieve positive, long -term behavioral change
  • Create an environment where people can achieve maximum behavioral change while minimizing wasted time
Employee Engagement and Planning for the future:
  • How to engage your employees and stop wasting everyone's time?
  • Learn how to implement a new process for employee engagement that teaches employees how to engage themselves.
  • Planning for the Future: Be ready to use a new process for planning that can be applied to individuals, teams and organisation.      

Marshall will share the classic challenges faced by successful leaders and will illustrate how to use "What to Stop" in leadership development & coaching. You will then practice feedforward - a focused tool for change that has been successfully implemented by leaders around the World.
