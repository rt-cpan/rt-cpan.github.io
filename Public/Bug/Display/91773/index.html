NPN has been obsoleted by ALPN per the IETF; Google will be removing all support for NPN by the end of 2014 and it's looking like HTTP/2.0 will *require* ALPN advertisement in TLS per the latest few drafts. Given that the IETF has blessed ALPN and ALPN support is now in OpenSSL trunk, it's probably time to add it to Net::SSLeay alongside NPN.
