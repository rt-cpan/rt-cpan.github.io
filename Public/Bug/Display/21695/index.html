Hello, It's not a bug. Everything works in your script as soon as the following intruction is inserted in the beginning of the loop, i.e. before getTableRows(): $doc->normalizeSheet($sheet, 30, 6); Knowing that OpenOffice.org Calc can store many neighbour cells with equal values in a single XML element, and that the used area of a spreadsheet is unknown, normalizeSheet() is a pre-processing method wich rebuilds the XML storage in order to prepair a rectangular area where the direct cell addressing safely works. For more details, I recommend you to read the getTable() and normalizeSheet() sections in the OpenOffice::OODoc::Text manual chapter http://search.cpan.org/dist/OpenOffice-OODoc/OODoc/Text.pod Le Lun.
