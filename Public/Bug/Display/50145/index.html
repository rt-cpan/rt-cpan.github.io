Yes. If Build.PL adds directories to @INC for its build subclass, these do not get recalled during current() as they would with new_from_context(). perl -Iinc -MModule::Build -e 'print Module::Build->current;'

text/plain 338b
Module::Build->resume() adds _build/lib to @INC for resuming a subclass, but does not add any other directories that might have been added to @INC during Build.PL (e.g. 'inc'). @INC additions should be saved in config and used during resume. Reported by brian d foy: http://www.nntp.perl.org/group/perl.module.build/2009/09/msg2542.html
