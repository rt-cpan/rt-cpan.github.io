Aha, you're not accounting for leap seconds, no doubt. There's some info on this in the docs (http://search.cpan.org/~drolsky/DateTime-0.4304/lib/DateTime.pm#Leap_Seconds_and_Date_Math). The short answer is use the floating time zone for things on which you do math, and leap seconds will be ignored. -dave /*========================== VegGuide.Org Your guide to all that's veg ==========================*/
