Hello, On my SVN installation, users use their X509 certificate to authenticate, and therefore their logins are in the user@FQDN format (e-mail address). It seems that when SVN::Log falls back to the command-line mode, it can't parse 'svn log' output because first line of each commit contains those logins. To fix this, it seems that line 212 should become --- my $headrule = qr/r(\d+) \| \w+ \| (\d{4}-\d{2}-\d{2} \d{2}:\d{2}:\d{2})/; +++ my $headrule = qr/r(\d+) \| ([\w@\.]+) \| (\d{4}-\d{2}-\d{2} \d{2}:\d{2}:\d{2})/; to accept e-mail addresses as login. Hope this helps !
