  Tue Nov 01 18:36:42 2011 mschwern [...] cpan.org - Correspondence added
MakeMaker puts in that #! line, see ExtUtils::MM_Unix->_fixin_replace_shebang. It uses %Config to know what to do. $Config{startperl}, $Config{perlpath} and $Config{sharpbang}. If those are incorrect, it won't know any better. Please check those out.
