I've sent this patch to Les, the Number::Spell maintainer, three times in the last year. I've received no response. The patch includes the following: Bugfixes in test.pl: * Fixed a typo in the EU tests * Turned on the EU tests, which were mistakenly never performed Enhancements in Spell.pm and test.pl: * MANY more tests in test.pl * support for zero and for negative numbers * limited but useful support for floating point numbers My company has used this patch on a web site for six months to write out numbers on simulated checks for online payment. It works very well for us. I would be happy to take over maintenance of this module, since the author seems unwilling to respond to bug reports.
