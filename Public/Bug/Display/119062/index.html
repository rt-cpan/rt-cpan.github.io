Subject: List::Util::uniq evaulation context
The attached test case utluniqtest.pl suggests that utl->uniq always evaluates in the scalar context: Source - One Fish Two Fish Red Fish Blue Fish List::Util::uniq - One Fish Two Red Blue utl->uniq - 5 coerce A - 5 coerce B - 5 utl->shuffle - Blue Fish Fish One Fish Red Two Fish utl->shuffle works as expected, indicating that uniq is behaving differently from other List::Util functions.
