Number::Format doesn't support a multiple character thousands separator; however, it's simple to implement. Would you mind accepting the attached patch to add support for it? It includes a new test and a POD update. If I'm missing another reason why this shouldn't be supported, please let me know. Thanks for your consideration. Best, Nick Patch
