Subject: Loads and loads of warnings when executing more than 100,000 test points
Hello Andy, As we discussed on IRC, Test::Harness prints screens of warnings when executing a test script with more than 100,000 test points: Use of uninitialized value in numeric gt (>) at /usr/lib/perl5/5.8.5/Test/Harness/Straps.pm line 188, <FILE> line 100001. Enormous test number seen [test 100001] Can't detailize, too big. As promised, I won't argue concerning than limit :-) However, printing loads and loads of warnings is of no use. Plus the warning reveals a potential problem. Attached to this ticket is a patch to Test::Harness::Straps that corrects this warning and avoid printing the "too many tests" warnings once it has been printed. Regards
