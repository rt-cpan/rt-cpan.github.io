Subject: Cache-Memcached-1.29: perl 5.12 changes break IPv6 detection
Perl 5.12 appears to have moved AF_INET6 and PF_INET6 constants into Socket.pm and conditionally out of Socket6.pm if Socket.pm exports them. This breaks Cache::Memcached's detection of IPv6 support. Patch is included below.
