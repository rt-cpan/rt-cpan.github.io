In addition, our Rail Services and Inspection Technologies division is a leader in non-destructive testing solutions for railroads. We continue to develop machines and services by working closely with our customers to create innovative new products. Therefore, we are pleased to announce three new products. Auto Spike Feeder The Auto Spike Feeder (ASF) is an option for Nordco’s CX Hammer Production Spiker; existing CX Hammers can also be retrofitted. The ASF moves spikes seamlessly from the bulk bin to the driving guns, without operator intervention. For teams that use a third crew member to feed spikes, the ASF enables the CX Hammer to operate with only two crew members; if operators are currently feeding spikes themselves, the ASF reduces the distractions that arise from loading spikes during machine operation. Nordstar Proximity Awareness System The Nordstar system alerts maintenance-of-way operators if machines are approaching each other too quickly for conditions or spacing requirements. Nordstar uses on-board GPS and a high-frequency radio to identify and transmit a machine’s exact position, heading, and speed to other machines in the area. The information is used to calculate the location of nearby machines and activate an alarm when machine moves into the critical spacing zone. Hitch-Mounted Rail Inspection System The hitch-mounted rail inspection system mounts to most hi-rail vehicles with a Class III hitch, allowing track inspection teams to expand their track inspection capabilities during a single pass. The system uses the same patented wheel probe technology and digital signal processing as all other Nordco rail inspection equipment. The complete system includes the inspection carriage, water tank and air compressor, generator, and a computer with real-time data collection.
