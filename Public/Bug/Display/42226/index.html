Hi, fist of all, I like to express how I enjoyed playing with Net::BGP - thank you very much for this useful framework/module. I think i found a bug in Update.pm, in the way the AGGREGATOR attribute gets constructed at _encode_aggregator. inet_aton will output an packet format/struct already, so its not required to pack it again. Please see the diff below. I tested this behavior with a BGP adjacency and can confirm that advertising routes with AGGREGATOR only works when I apply the patch (verified with packet captures and debugging at the peer).
