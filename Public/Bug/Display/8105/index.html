And yet perldoc Test::Reporter fails. The problem is that perldoc looks for either .pod documents (the Real POD, the source from which nroff documents/HTML/LaTeX/whatever are generated), or for documentation within the module. If that's not installed then perldoc won't work. Have you tried running "perldoc Test::Reporter" after installation? Regards, Shlomi Fish Show quoted text
