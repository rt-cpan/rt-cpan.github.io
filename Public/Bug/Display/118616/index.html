The zero argument to test_harness() explicitly sets the verbosity to zero; thus overriding HARNESS_VERBOSE=1. I think I'd call this is an ExtUtils::MakeMaker issue, not a Test::Harness one. `make test TEST_VERBOSE=1` should DWIM anyway, on old and new MakeMakers. Leon
