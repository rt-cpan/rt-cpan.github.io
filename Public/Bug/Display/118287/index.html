The conference seeks submissions from academia, government, and industry presenting novel research results in various topics on human sustainability.   **SCOPE** Topics of interest for INSAN 2016 include, but are not limited to :  **1. Education, Language and Communication** *          Human Sustainability Curriculum *          Human Development Through Co-Curricular Activities *          Characters Formation  *          Emotional and Spiritual Intelligence  *          Education and Patriotism *          Teacher's Role in Human Capital Formation  *          Roles of School in Human Sustainability  *          Social Media and Media Communication *          Language and Community Learning  *          Issues and Challenges in Education, Language and Communication for Human Sustainability Enhancement ** ** **2. Science and Technology** *          Policy and Implementation of Technology in Human Development  *          Technology Application for Practices *          ICT for Community Development  *          IT Literacy in Modern Community  *          Technology for Innovation and Creativity  *          Training and Development for Individual and Organization  *          E-learning for Public Sector  *          The Impact of Science and Technology  *          Issues and Challenges in Science and Technology for Human Sustainability Enhancement   **4. History and Civilization** *          Islamic Philosophy and Thought  *          Faith and Islamic Law (Syariah)  *          Famous Figures and Thought - Preaching and Leadership  *          Islamic Economy and Transactions  *          Muslim Morals and Professionalism  *          Science and Technology in Islam  *          Contemporary Issues in History and Civilization Studies for Human Sustainability Enhancement   We invite you to attend INSAN 2016 and submit full papers in English or Malay (maximum 10 pages including notes and references), through online submission ;
