But the thing won't show up if everything is green or red, so I prefer to start with the matrix. On the matrix for 5.09 you see a link to "Reports analysis": http://analysis.cpantesters.org/solved?distv=Tapper-Reports-Web-5.0.9 At the moment, the correlation about a thing called "mod:DateTime::Format::Strptime" is listed as the third and when you click on that you're navigated down on the same page because it is an anchor you clicked on.
