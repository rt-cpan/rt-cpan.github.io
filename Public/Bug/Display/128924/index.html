Do you have big goals for your community? Maybe you want to provide art education to underprivileged youth? Protect local wildlife? Improve financial literacy? or maybe NIH or NSF grants. We can help you get there.

Learn to find funders who share your ideas, plan logical programs, and win the grant funding to make it all real. This workshop presents actionable advice on how to plan and write proposals that win grants.
