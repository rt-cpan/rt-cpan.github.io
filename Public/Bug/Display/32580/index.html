This may actually be a problem with Time::Piece rather than Time::Piece::MySQL. On MacOS X 10.5.7, I altered Piece.xs in Time::Piece to use strptime_l instead of strptime (the strptime manpage mentions legacy handling of %Y) and compiled and installed - Time::Piece still passes its tests. Time::Piece::MySQL then compiled and passed its tests without modification. I guess a couple of #ifdefs for OS X version in Time::Piece Piece.xs would probably suffice - let me know if you want more details.
