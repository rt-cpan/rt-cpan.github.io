  Fri Apr 29 22:15:31 2011 ribasushi [...] leporine.io - Correspondence added
Yes this is a documented caveat. Currently our search-specification tools (SQL::abstract) are not mature enough to offer introspection of the structure, and as such it is very hard to figure out what is a column and what is an alias/accessor and needs to be substituted for a "real" column name. I am rejecting this ticket as "wontfix" as the resolution of this issue is nowhere near to being solved in the short term.
