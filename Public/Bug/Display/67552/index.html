Thank you for the report, Yogesh. The TSTP warnings were unexpected to me -- I'll fix the test in v0.51 to suppress those warnings. And I'll leave this ticket open until the fix is released. All of the other warnings you see are normal. Many of the tests are exercising the distribution in either invalid and inconsistent ways (for example, setting a timeout of -5 seconds). Other tests probe the normal failure modes of the distribution (letting a child process timeout, having a call to the system's fork() fail and return undef, etc.). It is correct behavior for the tests to produce warnings in these cases. -- Marty O'Brien
