There are a handful of distributions in CPAN that have bad $VERSION assignments (I scanned and found 8). Odds are your problem is Mail::SpamAssassin (the rest are much more obscure): https://issues.apache.org/SpamAssassin/show_bug.cgi?id=6422 There's already a new release of Module::Load::Conditional that protects against the breakage you've reported, so if you upgrade to 0.38, you should be able to proceed as usual.
