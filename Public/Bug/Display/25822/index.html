Subject: Tcl on Cygwin
Guess I'm the first to try your module under Cygwin. The attached patch contains 3 changes: 1. Makefile.PL uses tclConfig.sh under Cygwin. 2. Uses INT2PTR in Tcl.xs to silence a few compiler warnings. 3. Works around an apparent deficiency in canonpath() for Cygwin in info.t. Looking forward to using your module. Thanks.
