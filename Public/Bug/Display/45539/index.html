Subject: empty tags like <img src="foo"> and <br /> are output incorrectly with a closing tag
perl -MpQuery -le 'print pQuery("<html><body><img src=\"/test.html\" /></body></html>")->find("img")->toHtml;' <img /="/" src="/test.html"></img> Notice the extra /="/" attribute and the extra </img> closing tag. The attached patch uses HTML::Tagset to find out which tags have no closing tag and outputs them appropriately. After the patch, the above is rendered like this: <img src="/test.html" /> Cheers, Cees Hek
