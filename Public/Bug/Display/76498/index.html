  Wed Apr 11 14:45:41 2012 rt-cpan [...] trout.me.uk - Ticket created
Subject: META.json nor POD provides bugtracker URL
Your META.json does not set the bugtracker resource, and nor does your documentation provide a procedure for filing bugs. As such users are unable to determine where to contact you with problems - and since it's normally considered rude to directly email the author of a distribution, the natural default is sending to rt.cpan.org. Assuming you do not wish to have users report bugs via rt.cpan.org, please set the appropriate field - something like META_MERGE => { resources => { bugtracker => 'mailto:mlehmann@cpan.org' } } in your WriteMakefile() call will do the trick - and since it is, after all, just a URL, if you prefer direct email I see no reason you can't use a mailto URL (if this turns out to confuse certain systems I will happily chase down the bugs in those, since I'm the one who cares about that part) Note that http://metacpan.org/release/Plack and http://search.cpan.org/dist/Plack demonstrate that the field is currently honoured by the two main online module access systems, so by setting the META.json field correctly and by providing a note in the documentation indicating where to send bugs to, you can substantially reduce the chances of getting reports via rt.cpan.org by mistake. As things stand, however, the complete lack of any direction on your part leaves us unable to determine any other approach than the CPAN standard, which is to report to rt.cpan.org; if this is not what you desire, then please tell your users. I'd be happy to write the POD section describing the bug reporting process and to submit this along with an appropriate META_MERGE change as a patch if you'd like.
