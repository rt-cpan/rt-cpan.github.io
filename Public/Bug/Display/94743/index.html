Dear Authors: Martin and Juan Jose, I thank you very much for your effort to publish the perl module Email-Send-SMTP-Gmail. I simply love it :) There is, however, a little bug that bothers me - a missing date field in the email header. Below you can see the the message that the mail server inserts into the email header: X-Amavis-Alert: BAD HEADER SECTION, Missing required header field: "Date" It complicates things a lot as some email clients like Thunderbird sort the message with the default date of the year 1970 way down the list of email messages. And I need it badly to properly and effectively communicate with my chemistry students sending them some homework assignments in attachment. I know Perl a bit but the module looks to complex for me. Could you help me please with this problem? Thank you in advance!
