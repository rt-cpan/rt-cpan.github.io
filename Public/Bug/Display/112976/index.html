Subject: Recent Crypt::ECB versions break other CPAN modules
It seems that Crypt::ECB 2.10 and 2.05 (maybe also 2.00) break the test suites or compilation of other CPAN modules depending on Crypt::ECB. I found the following problems: - WebService-Pandora-0.3 https://rt.cpan.org/Ticket/Display.html?id=112971 - Validate-Yubikey-0.01 https://rt.cpan.org/Ticket/Display.html?id=112974 - Net-Telnet-Netgear-0.04 https://github.com/Robertof/perl-net-telnet-netgear/issues/3 - Crypt-PWSafe3-1.21 https://rt.cpan.org/Ticket/Display.html?id=112975
