Subject: Buglets in DJabberd::Authen::LDAP
There are a couple of buglets in DJabberd::Authen::LDAP 0.02, fixed in the attached patch. The first causes D::A::L to fail with "Invalid LDAP Authentication method" if LDAPMethod is explicitly set to 'rebind', instead of being omitted. I don't remember the impact of the unbind vs. bind, but bind() looks the correct call, I think?
