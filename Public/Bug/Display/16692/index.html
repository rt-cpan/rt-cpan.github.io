Subject: Email obfuscation improvement suggestion
Just a tip to improve the obfuscation just a bit. If you mix in some div tags, you can really muddle up a harvester. foo.bar@example.com fo&lt;div style="display: none; visibility: hidden;"&gt; some-random-meaningless-string &lt;/div&gt;o.bar@&lt;div style="display: none; visibility: hidden;"&gt; new-and-different-random-meaningless-string &lt;/div&gt;examp&lt;div style="display: none; visibility: hidden;"&gt; yet-another-different-random-meaningless-string &lt;/div&gt;le.c&lt;div style="display: none; visibility: hidden;"&gt; one-more-different-random-meaningless-string &lt;/div&gt;om Note the inclusion of the div tags in the middle of words, not just at the regular word boundaries.
