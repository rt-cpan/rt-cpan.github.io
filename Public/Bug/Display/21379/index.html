Subject: [Patch+Test] TAPx::Parser::Streamed has leftovers from previous (unrelated) invocations
Download (untitled) / with headers
text/plain 559b
The test file in this patch demonstrates a case where TAPx::Parser::Stream is called, and then stops processing after a bailout, and afterwards a different parser is initialised and gets some of its leftover tokens. This caused a subtle bug during the conversion of Test::Run to use TAPx::Parser. The patch also corrects the problem, by converting two "static" variables into object variables. I've noticed other places where such static variables are used, and they may cause similar problems due to their Singleton nature. Regards, Shlomi Fish
