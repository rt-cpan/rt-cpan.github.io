Thanks! ------8<-----------8<-----------8<-----------8<-----------8<----- Package: libmail-srs-perl Version: 0.31-5 Severity: normal Tags: upstream Dear Maintainer, the forward subroutine of SRS.pm contains a relatively simple RegEx to match the local-part and domain-part of the original mail sender.
