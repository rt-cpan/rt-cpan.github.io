The author meant the link to go to a real section also named "Decimal Versions" a few lines later: https://metacpan.org/pod/version::Internals#Decimal-Versions1 Unfortunately, the Pod specification is vague about the right behavior of linking to same-named sections. perlpodspec.pod says: This specification does not specify what behavior should be in the case of a given document having several things all seeming to produce the same section identifier (A section may be a =head or =item here) Different renderers behave differently here. tkpod prefers to link to =head sections first and would create the "correct" link. The metacpan pod2html renderer seems to choose the first such section, even if it is linking to the same section (which could be constructed as a bug, or at least a behavior which could be done better).
