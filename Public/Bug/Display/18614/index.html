Thanks, Cindy Jett Mapmuse.com 1326 14th Street NW Washington, DC 20005 Hi, I am writing to let bingo enthusiasts know about a website that has interactive mapping of bingo halls, and regular bingo events (for example, weekly bingo night at your neighborhood social club or church) across the US. In order to view the Bingo Halls Map, see the following page: http://find.mapmuse.com/re1/interest.php?brandID=BINGO_HALLS The idea is for bingo enthusiasts to build upon what we have started by adding bingo halls, and regular bingo events that we have missed, and editing and enhancing information that is already there. Through this kind of community effort, we hope to have the most comprehensive, descriptive, and up to date maps to help people find bingo events at home and on their travels. We would appreciate if you would participate in the process, by checking if your local bingo halls and events are mapped, enhancing information as you see fit, and adding places that we have missed. This is simple to do. To add a bingo hall or bingo event, go to the Bingo Halls map, click on “Know any bingo halls we are missing?”, fill out the form, click submit and your information will be posted within a few hours. To edit information about a bingo hall that is already mapped, click on the icon associated with the bingo hall. An information box will pop up, click the edit link, fill out the form, click submit and the changes will appear within a few hours. If you find our services useful, we would appreciate it if you could mention us if you have a blog, newsletter or website related to bingo. Ofcourse, a link from your website would also be greatly appreciated, as would passing the word about MapMuse onto like-minded friends.
