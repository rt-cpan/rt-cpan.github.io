Dear Dorian Taylor, Hi. This is imacat from Taiwan. I found that the Makefile.PL in your Apache-LogF-0.01 die() when mod_perl version is insane. I would like to suggest you to exit() with 0 instead of die() in your Makefile.PL, as suggested by this article: <<Notes For CPAN Authors> "How can I stop getting FAIL reports for missing libraries or other non-Perl dependencies?" http://cpantest.grango.org/wiki/CPANAuthorNotes I made a simple patch to Apache-LogF-0.01, in the hope that it helps. Please tell me if you have any question, or if I could be of any help. Thank you.
