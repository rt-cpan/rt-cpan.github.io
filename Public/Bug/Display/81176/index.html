I would like to report a bug in WWW/HtmlUnit.pm, which causes it to fail on Windows. Essentially, HtmlUnit.pm assumes that the classpath separator is ':'. But on Windows, it's ';', because ':' conflicts with the names of disks like C:/windows. The fix is to add this at the beginning of HtmlUnit.pm our $classpath_separator = $^O =~ /win/i ? ";" : ":"; and then use $classpath_separator instead of ':' everywhere where ':' is used to concatenate jar pathes together.
