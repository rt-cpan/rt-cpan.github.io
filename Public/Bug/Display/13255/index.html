That's naughty. That actually sets $IFS, the input field separator, for subsequently launched /bin/sh programs. Here's a simple patch that fixes the problem: ----8<---- --- Cwd.pm~ 2005/05/05 11:07:31 +++ Cwd.pm 2005/06/14 11:08:07 @@ -292,7 +292,8 @@ # The 'natural and safe form' for UNIX (pwd may be setuid root) sub _backtick_pwd { - local @ENV{qw(PATH IFS CDPATH ENV BASH_ENV)}; + my @localize = grep exists $ENV{$_}, qw(PATH IFS CDPATH ENV BASH_ENV); + local @ENV{@localize}; my $cwd = `$pwd_cmd`; # Belt-and-suspenders in case someone said "undef $/". local $/ = "\n"; ---->8---- Cheers, Neil
