> =item -[T|template] [=] <template> > =item -[R|delete] <PARAM>
This will not work. [] indicates that what is inside is optional. Try: =item -T [=] <template> | --template [=] <template> =item -R <PARAM> | --delete <PARAM> The other issues you bring up appear to be solved in 0.2.0.
