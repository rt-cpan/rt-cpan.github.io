Subject: Got it to work and blew up in the link
I managed to get MQSeries to compile under Strawberry 5.18 with two tweaks. One is described in https://rt.cpan.org/Ticket/Display.html?id=88555 . The other is tweak to the Makefile.PL in MQClient/MQServer (they're linked): if ($Config{osname} =~ /win32/i and $Config{myuname} =~ m/strawberry-perl/ and $Config{myuname} =~ m/x64/ and $Config{cc} eq 'gcc') { $Config{ccflags} .= ' -D_int64=__int64' } That gets me through the compile.
