Subject: Wide characters at subroutine entry
If I retrieve firstname from gmail via attribute exchange extension, I receive error "Wide characters at subroutine entry", because my name is Андрiй and Digest::SHA1::sha1 does not know what to do with that characters :-) I've uploaded a patch for it. Also I've switched Crypt::DH dependency to Crypt::DH::GMP (wrong deps of Crypt::DH enrage me).
