Subject: Error encoding booleans after Storable and Data::Dump have been called
This is a weird one. I've attached a test script that will crash with: cannot encode reference to scalar 'JSON::XS::Boolean=SCALAR(0x184d768)' unless the scalar is 0 or 1 at json-xs-bool.pl line 21. However if you comment out line 14: warn "From JSON: " . dump($from) . "\n"; The script will run fine. It also works fine if you remove the use of Storable and simply call to_json on the $from value.
Subject: json-xs-bool.pl
#!/usr/bin/perl use strict; use lib qw(blib/lib); use JSON::XS 1.3 qw(from_json to_json); use Data::Dump qw(dump); use Storable qw(nfreeze thaw); my $json = qq{ {"this":true} }; my $from = from_json($json); warn "From JSON: " . dump($from) . "\n"; my $frozen = nfreeze($from); my $thawed = thaw($frozen); warn "Thawed: " . dump($thawed) . "\n"; my $to = to_json($thawed); warn "To JSON: " . dump($to) . "\n";
