Subject: Add support for <guid isPermaLink> in RSS 2.0 feeds
RSS 2.0 entries may contain <guid> elements with isPermaLink attribute. They get converted to 'permaLink' instead of 'guid'. Take this into account when returning links not only when setting. See included test.
