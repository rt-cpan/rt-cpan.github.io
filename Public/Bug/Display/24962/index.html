Hello Sinisa, Nice work on the CA::AutoSys module, it installed and ran very easily for me. Noticed a disparity between CA autorep (4.5) and your example autorep script, CA reports time in local time, you report in GMT. Fix is simple: $ diff lib/CA/AutoSys/Status.pm.orig lib/CA/AutoSys/Status.pm 80c80 < my ($sec, $min, $hour, $mday, $mon, $year, $wday, $yday) = gmtime($time); --- Show quoted text
> my ($sec, $min, $hour, $mday, $mon, $year, $wday, $yday) =
localtime($time); This makes the timestamps identical to CA's autorep, in our environment at least. Note also that the '-w' option is an undocumented option to CA autorep in AutoSys 4.5, it is equivalent to your '-w 116' option. Note also that you have hard-coded Sybase as the CA DB, they support others including Oracle.
