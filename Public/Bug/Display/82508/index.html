It is a perfect time to reevaluate the way you have been raising funds for ongoing projects or goals. Tired of the same ol' ways of fundraising? Want something more for your time and effort? Adventures in Fundraising has a solution to help you easily raise funds for any goal, club, team, project, group or cause. It's fun, effective and simple! Five Dollar DOTS is an exciting way for you to earn more funds with less time and effort. Five Dollar DOTS is a digital fundraiser in which you sell digital DOTS to help you reach your goals. Each fundraiser is given an individual page and tracking. You can easily set up Five Dollar DOTS for your goal, free of charge, and start earning money instantly. No useless products, no deliveries, no inventory, just money! Provide us with the information we need to get you started and we will set it up for you and send you a login. Five Dollar DOTS will translate your financial goal into DOTS. We will upload an image of your goal that you provide (we can provide one for you also) and that image is covered in DOTS. As DOTS are purchased, your image or goal will slowly be revealed. Here is the best part...WE do all the major work, WE host your page and WE manage all transactions and YOU keep the majority of all funds! The traditional fundraiser, with overpriced or useless products, requires you to do all the work, manage the money, and they keep the majority of all the money earned. How does that benefit you? As opposed to the traditional "You keep 30% and we will take 70%," we will take the 30% and give you the 70%. That's 70% of ALL funds raised coming directly to you! No set up fees, no strings attached, we only keep 30% of anything earned. You will never owe us anything more!
