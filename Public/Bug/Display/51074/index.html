The synopsis erroneously indicate the output format of format_datetime will be 2003-01-16T23:12:01+0200 when it will in fact output 2003-01-16 23:12:01 The attached patch fixes this bug, and also adds support for parsing timestamps including the T. Also includes a unit test. -- Knut Arne Bjørndal, Tekniker Easy Connect AS - http://1890.no E-post: knut.arne.bjorndal@easyconnect.no Tlf: 53 20 53 25
