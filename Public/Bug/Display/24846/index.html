Subject: Does not correctly recurse into references in a PVNV-type scalar
I was working on optimizing the memory usage of a program, using Devel::Size to report on it, when I noticed that a particular change made for a huge decrease in memory! When I examined it further, I realized that Devel::Size was sometimes telling me that my hashes were smaller than their contents. After much careful analysis with Devel::Peek, I determined that the problem was this: the nature of my program produced hash values that were PVNV-types (even though they stored references). When Devel::Size would go to recurse and find the size of the hash's elements, it doesn't notice the ROK flag and thus does not recurse into the reference.
