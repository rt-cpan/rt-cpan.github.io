Subject: dlock("0") locks $0
Try `perl -e 'use Data::Lock qw(dlock); dlock "0"; $0 = "hoge"'`, which will fail. "no strict 'refs'" seems the fault. "use strict 'refs'" again in the (un)lock routines. -- Goro Fuji (gfx) GFUJI at CPAN.org

Thanks. unfortunately that solution fails to lock ordinary non-reference data types. I also found d(un)?lock loops forever on circular reference. the patch below fix all that. New version on its way to CPAN. Dan On Wed Mar 20 10:28:49 2013, GFUJI wrote: Show quoted text
