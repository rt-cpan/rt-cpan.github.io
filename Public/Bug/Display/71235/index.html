Hi John, The version of Perl IBM is advertising there, 5.8.7, was released in May of 2005 and is currently 3 major releases out of date. As a community, perl5-porters isn't equipped to support EBCDIC on our own. As far as we can tell, nobody actively involved in the community has experience with z/OS or access to z/OS. Do you know if IBM has resources dedicated to maintaining Perl for z/OS? We'd be absolutely thrilled to engage with them to help ensure that Perl works well on z/OS, but without some involvement from IBM, it'd be quite difficult for us to do so.
