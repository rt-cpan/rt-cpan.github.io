text/plain 415b
At least in RT 3.5.7 (probably even in all of 3.5.x) TitleBox, TitleBoxStart and TitleBoxEnd have moved from $RT_HOME/html/share/Elements to $RT_HOME/html/share/Widgets. This causes errors when accessing any of the RightsMatrix pages. Simply replacing any occurences of "Elements/TitleBox" with "Widgets/TitleBox" fixes this (tested in RT 3.5.7). RightsMatrix really helps with RT rights management, thanks a lot!
