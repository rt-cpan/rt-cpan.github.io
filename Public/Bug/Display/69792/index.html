Dear "cplusplus programmer", You are spamming your help requests to the wrong places. This is not going to get you the answers you want and pisses off people that have nothing to do with the problem you're having. If you are having problems with a specific library on CPAN, generally *only* the author is going to be able to help you. If you are having problems installing *any* library from CPAN, then it sounds like you need help configuring your CPAN client. Let me suggest you join the perl-beginner's mailing list and ask your questions there: http://learn.perl.org/faq/beginners.html Other good resources for searching for answers or asking beginner questions include Perlmonks http://perlmonks.org/ and StackOverflow http://stackoverflow.com/ Good luck, David
