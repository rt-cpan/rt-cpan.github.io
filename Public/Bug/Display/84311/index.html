Right now new tickets pull their subject from the original ticket and they are the same. It shouldn't be too hard to add something to the subject. The due date is available in RT, but I suppose having the date in the subject might be useful in email. Another option would be to create a template that includes the due date (in the subject or email body) for On Create for certain tickets. Thanks for the suggestion. I think having some options to add something to the subject to differentiate tickets, especially when you might have multiple active at once, might be a useful feature in the future. The question is what to put there, date being one option.
