Attend this one-day intensive course and understand all of the critical
“need-to-know” power and gas industry components.

EMI's Fundamentals of Power & Gas course, held May 23, in Weehawken, NJ (5 minutes to New York City), will introduce you to the dynamic and evolving natural gas and power industries.

Get Course Syllabus    Register

The course describes the natural gas and power industries across four major functions:

  1. Origins and destinations of natural gas and power.
  2. Exploration through distribution.
  3. Buying, selling and marketing.
  4. Transportation.

Specific topics covered include:

  • Overview of U.S. natural gas and power market macro- and micro-fundamentals.
  • State of the power industry.
  • Power transmission – electron flow from the plant to the end user.
  • Power economics.
  • FERC orders and their impact on the natural gas industry.
  • The players and where they fit in the natural gas infrastructure.
  • Transportation: moving natural gas to market.
  • Traditional buying techniques.

Get detailed course info here or register. We look forward to seeing you in New Jersey.
