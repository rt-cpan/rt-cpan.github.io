Apologies: I'm new to submitting patches and such but I've attached a patch-file created using diff -u old new - I hope that this is useful. I've attached my new version as well in case that is more useful. I also think that I've managed to put my version through the test suite that CPAN downloads alongside your module. I performed a get, a make and a test, edited the version under ~/.cpan and then ran another test: all passed.
